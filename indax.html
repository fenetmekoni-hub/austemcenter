About STEM Synergy

STEM Synergy Inc. is a U.S.-based international NGO, also registered in Canada, dedicated to advancing STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) education, entrepreneurship, and social enterprises in Ethiopia. Founded in August 2016 G.C. by passionate Ethiopians in the U.S. and Canada, alongside Jewish philanthropist Mark Gelfand, STEM Synergy builds on the legacy of the Gelfand Family Charitable Trust (GFCT) in promoting STEM education and technology-driven solutions.

  • Promoting STEM education for young generation.
  • Facilitating technology transfer to solve real societal problems.
  • Supporting social enterprises for sustainable economic growth.

STEM Synergy empowers Ethiopian youth through STEM education, technology transfer, and social enterprises, fostering innovation, sustainability, and economic opportunities.

STEM Centers

In STEM Center labs, students gain in-depth enrichment, beyond their regular schoolwork. Experienced mentors guide the students to design-build experiments, circuitry, and practical machines.

Science and Engineering Fairs

STEM Synergy Inc. organizes and sponsors local and national Science and Engineering Fairs in partnership with Ethiopia’s Ministry of Education and STEM stakeholders, hosting nine annual events to date.

Entrepreneurship Training

STEM Synergy trains small business groups and STEM graduates in entrepreneurship, boosting growth and career readiness.
